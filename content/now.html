Currently, I'm a Software Engineer at ING Netherlands. I work in the MDPL team that's responsible for tracing, metrics and logs; also known as observability (o11y).

Specifically working on a marketplace of metrics, where teams can expose certain metrics in a defined format, for other teams to use.

We use Grafana (with Mimir), OpenTelemetry and Prometheus and Go as the language. We have built custom controllers to synchronize our agents, one of which is a custom one for Prometheus Recording Rules.
